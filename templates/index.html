What is shelfi?
A brief overview of the application
Overview
shelfi is a collaborative pantry and recipe management application that helps households and groups manage food inventory and meal planning.
Core features
- Create and join shared spaces for collaborative management
- Add, track, and organize pantry items with real-time updates
- Share recipes and receive suggestions based on available ingredients
Benefits
- Reduce food waste
- Streamline grocery shopping
- Enhance meal preparation with ingredient-based suggestions
